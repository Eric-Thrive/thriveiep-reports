Executive Summary

Key Messages

  • Most commercial soft skills tools cannot prove they work. Only 3 of 14 publish any psychometric validation. C2A uses 14 independently validated instruments with published reliability and validity evidence.
  • No competitor tracks student growth over time. All 14 commercial tools are single-timepoint hiring assessments. C2A's 3-tier longitudinal cadence is unique in the market.
  • Coaching integration is C2A's strongest differentiator. Zero commercial tools connect assessment to coaching workflows. C2A's DDx-style reporting gives coaches actionable differential diagnosis, backed by 19 positive-outcome studies.

What did we study?

We reviewed 48 papers across 6 research databases to compare C2A against 14 commercial soft skills tools: iMocha, Hire Success, Toggl Hire, Mettl, Bryq, TestGorilla, eSkill, Neuroworx, Clevry, Pymetrics/Harver, Merittrac, JobArch, HighMatch, and Retorio.

What did we find?

C2A occupies a fundamentally different category. The competitors are hiring screeners (single-timepoint, single-method, no coaching). C2A is a longitudinal coaching-integrated developmental platform for neurodivergent students. The literature validates every architectural choice in the battery design.

How strong is this evidence?

3 HIGH (meta-analyses, systematic reviews), 10 MODERATE (RCTs, multi-sample validations), 17 LOW (cross-sectional), 18 VERY_LOW (vendor documentation). Strongest evidence for multi-instrument battery design and ADHD coaching effectiveness. Weakest: commercial tool validation (most publish nothing).

Commercial Tool Comparison

14 tools classified into four tiers by publicly available psychometric evidence.

Tier 1 Published Data

  • Pymetrics/Harver - 12 neuroscience games, r=0.5 convergent, r=0.68 test-retest. Company-affiliated authors.
  • Clevry - 46 scales, alpha 0.65-0.91, validated vs BPI/16PF/CPI/MBTI. Most rigorous.

Tier 2 Framework Only

  • Mercer|Mettl - Proprietary MPM, global norms, IO psychologists. No independent validation.
  • TestGorilla - UGESP framework, alpha, DIF. No criterion validity.
  • HighMatch - IO psychologist-led. Client-validated only.

Tier 3 Bolt-On Soft Skills

  • iMocha - 2,500+ tests, primarily technical.
  • eSkill - 70K questions, hard skills focus.
  • Merittrac - Indian market. No validation.
  • Toggl Hire - Quiz templates. No framework.

Tier 4 Problematic

  • Retorio - AI video. Scores change with glasses/headscarves. HireVue dropped this 2021.
  • Neuroworx - Zero published validation.
  • JobArch - Not assessment. Free Italian ATS.
  • Hire Success - No published methodology.

Section 04 of 11

Assessment Methodology

C2A uses 17 instruments across 4 coaching lenses. The literature strongly supports multi-instrument approaches.

Multi-method assessment is empirically required

No single method meets all quality criteria (Baartman et al., 2007; 306 cites) MODERATE. The ABCD Study (Barch et al., 2017; 846 cites) is the gold standard for battery design HIGH.

Game-based assessments fall short

Meta-analysis: moderate-to-low convergent validity between GBA and traditional measures (Fadillah, 2025) HIGH. Best GBA study: r=0.5 convergent, r=0.68 test-retest (Leutner, 2023) LOW. Traditional convergence exceeds r=0.7.

Game-based tools score well on user experience (NPS 58) but sacrifice psychometric precision.

AI video assessment has fundamental problems

AVI models trained on self-reports show "little evidence of reliability or validity" (Hickman, 2021; JAP, 144 cites) MODERATE. Retorio scores changed with glasses, headscarves, backgrounds (Bogen & Rieke, 2022) MODERATE.

Measurement invariance is prerequisite

You cannot compare pre/post scores without proving the test measures the same thing at both time points (Knekta, 2019; 590 cites) HIGH. Most commercial tools do not report this.

Treatment sensitivity differs from reliability

Instruments can be reliable yet insensitive to coaching change (Benoy, 2019) MODERATE. C2A must select for sensitivity, not just consistency.

Section 06 of 11

Coaching Integration

No commercial tool integrates assessment with coaching. All 14 generate static reports without coaching workflows.

19/19 ADHD coaching studies show positive outcomes

Ahmann et al. (2018) reviewed all published ADHD coaching research. Every study showed improvement MODERATE.

RCT: coaching with validated instruments works

Field et al. (2013): coaching group scored significantly higher on all LASSI clusters (Skill, Will, Self-Regulation) MODERATE. These map to C2A's lens framework.

EF coaching fosters self-determination

Parker & Boutelle (2009; 155 cites) linked EF coaching to self-determination in college LD/ADHD students LOW - C2A's exact population.

Formative assessment needs its own validity framework

The key inference is utilization - how coaches use data for decisions (Gu, 2021) MODERATE. This grounds C2A's DDx reporting.

Section 09 of 11

Gaps & Limitations

  • No research on DDx-style coaching assessment reporting. C2A's approach is unprecedented.
  • No validation for 10 of 14 commercial tools.
  • No longitudinal soft skills measurement with multi-tier cadences.
  • Very limited neurodivergent-specific research on soft skills assessment.
  • GBA literature is hiring-only. No validation in developmental coaching.
  • No research on assessment-to-coaching feedback loops.

Publication Opportunity

C2A's use case is underrepresented. A validation study covering battery design + longitudinal cadence + coaching integration would fill multiple gaps.

What This Means for Us

Build

Critical Document the Kane Validity Argument

Use Kinnear et al. (2021) validity map methodology. Map scoring, generalization, extrapolation, utilization inferences. Most important step for institutional credibility.

Critical Establish measurement invariance

Knekta et al. (2019; 590 cites): cannot claim longitudinal tracking without proving invariance. Run testing on T1/T3 instruments before marketing.

High Verify treatment sensitivity

Per Benoy et al. (2019), reliability is insufficient. Pilot with pre/post data to find instruments insensitive to coaching change.

Moderate Publish a validation study

Covers battery design + longitudinal cadence + coaching integration. Establishes credibility. Differentiates from tools that publish nothing.

Avoid

  • No game-based assessment. Meta-analytic evidence: moderate-to-low convergent validity. Not justified for coaching.
  • No AI video analysis. Uniformly negative evidence for self-report constructs. Regulatory trajectory unfavorable.

Open Questions

  • How to handle social desirability bias in T3 self-report instruments?
  • What measurement invariance standard for neurodivergent populations?
  • Should C2A pursue micro-credentialing for Durable Skills (Thornhill-Miller, 2023; 561 cites)?
