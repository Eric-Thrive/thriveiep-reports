Measurement Invariance and Cohort Trends for SEL Measures across Four Statewide Administrations
ERIC Abulela et al. 2025, peer-reviewed

Assessed measurement invariance for 5 SEL measures across 4 statewide survey administrations (2013-2022, n=626,082 students). MI held across years per grade. Found large-effect declines in commitment to learning and school support from 2019-2022 (pre/post-COVID). Introduces RMSEA_D as a superior fit statistic for ordinal SEL items.

Relevant: Validates psychometric rigor at scale for SEL measures like our C2A battery. The RMSEA_D methodology could improve our instrument validation approach.
