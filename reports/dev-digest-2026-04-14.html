Document Processing & OCR

Jerry Liu: Visual grounding is the biggest gap in frontier model document OCR X

Frontier models (Gemini, Opus, GPT-5.4) lack spatial awareness of page element positions by default. LlamaIndex has made "massive strides" in segmenting and detecting granular elements in complex documents.

Relevant: Our GLM-OCR worker + PP-DocLayout pipeline addresses exactly this gap with layout-aware region cropping.

Automated PII Redaction on Drug Labels Using OCR and LLMs Semantic Scholar 1 citation

Two-stage pipeline using EasyOCR/Tesseract + Qwen2.5-72B achieves 92.5% precision, 87.6% F1, with only 3.1% over-redaction for regulatory compliance. Directly applicable to PII document redaction workflows.

Relevant: Validates our two-stage OCR+LLM redaction architecture. The 3.1% over-redaction rate is a useful baseline for our HIPAA FP work.

CDT: From Personalized to Programmed -- AI for IEPs Blog
